The Pre-Health Research Journal Club is a virtual student-run club at Hunter College. This journal club aims to provide a forum for individuals that are interested in discussing and presenting on published multifaceted research from various scientific journals. Journal club members present recent research that can cover wide ranges of topics0 relevant to all sciences. We also host speaker-series events led by researchers. All suggestions for topics, articles, speakers and volunteers that wish to present, are welcome. The mission of the Pre-Health Research Journal Club is to expose students to broad spectrums of interdisciplinary scientific research through critical analysis and presentation of current articles from scientific journals.
